Welcome to Meghalaya

About Meghalaya Hotels Things to Do

Meghalaya, meaning "abode of clouds", is a picturesque state located in the northeastern part of India. Known for its stunning landscapes, cascading waterfalls, and vibrant culture, Meghalaya offers a delightful escape for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. Explore the mesmerizing living root bridges, trek through lush green forests, and experience the unique Khasi and Jaintia cultures.

Cultural Beauty

Meghalaya is home to several indigenous tribes, each with its own rich cultural heritage. The Khasi, Garo, and Jaintia tribes celebrate traditional festivals, perform folk dances, and create unique handicrafts.

History

The history of Meghalaya dates back thousands of years, with evidence of ancient civilizations found in the region. It has been inhabited by various tribes and kingdoms, leaving behind a legacy of rich historical and archaeological sites.

Major Tourist Attractions

Some of the major tourist attractions in Meghalaya include the living root bridges of Cherrapunji, the Nohkalikai Falls, Mawsynram, the wettest place on Earth, and the sacred forests of Mawphlang.

Peak Time to Visit

The best time to visit Meghalaya is during the months of October to April, when the weather is pleasant and conducive for outdoor activities. However, each season offers its own unique experiences, so it depends on your preferences.

Things to Do in Meghalaya

  • Explore the Living Root Bridges
  • Visit Nohkalikai Falls
  • Discover Mawlynnong Village - Asia's Cleanest Village
  • Take a boat ride in Umngot River, Dawki
  • Experience the Nongriat Double Decker Living Root Bridge Trek
